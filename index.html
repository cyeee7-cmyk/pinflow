Questions?

We've got answers

Does PinFlow follow Pinterest's Terms of Service? ▾
Yes, completely. PinFlow uses Pinterest's official API exclusively. We don't use scraping, automation bots, or any method that violates Pinterest's ToS — your account is always safe.
Can I manage multiple Pinterest accounts? ▾
Yes. Pro supports up to 5 accounts and Agency supports up to 20, all from a single clean dashboard. Each account gets its own queue, analytics, and settings.
How does the AI description generator work? ▾
You give it an image, a link, or a topic. The AI generates a Pinterest-optimized title and description with relevant keywords — in seconds. No copywriting experience needed.
Is there really a free trial with no credit card? ▾
Absolutely. Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial, full-featured, no credit card required. You only get charged if you decide to continue after the trial ends.
Can I cancel anytime? ▾
Yes — no contracts, no lock-in. Cancel from your dashboard any time. Annual subscribers keep full access until the end of their billing period.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal. Payments are processed securely via Stripe.
